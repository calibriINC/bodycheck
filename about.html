BODYCHECK is the first data-driven self-experimentation app

About

Welcome to Bodycheck, the mobile app that empowers you to take control of your health and well-being by measuring and optimizing your digital biomarkers. Our platform enables you to conduct self-experiments and track your progress to improve your health, optimize your performance, and discover new insights about yourself.

Our app offers a range of features to help you achieve your health and performance goals. You can track your digital biomarkers, during and after experiments to identify which strategies work best for you. You can compare different interventions, such as diet and exercise routines, to determine which ones improves overall well-being.

In addition, you can adapt your experiments to optimize your performance. Our app helps you identify changes in your routine that lead to better performance and adjust your experiments accordingly. This allows you to test and track the effectiveness of new strategies and achieve your goals more efficiently.

With Bodycheck, you can take a personalized and data-driven approach to your health and well-being. Our app provides a powerful platform for conducting self-experiments and tracking their outcomes, giving you valuable insights into how to maximize your performance and achieve your full potential.
